Ventilation

We supply and install the following type of fans: Centrifugal, Centrifugal inline, axial, roof, propeller, jet.Prior to installation, we provide you with all the needed engineering studies and calculations to ensure proper operation of ventilation fans:

  • Technical selection
  • External static pressure calculations
  • Computational fluid dynamics calculations for parking areas
  • Noise studies for VIP projects

Air Conditioning

We supply and install the following type of air conditioning systems: Chillers, VRV/VRF, split units, multi split units, coolers. Prior to installation, we provide you with all the needed engineering studies and calculations to ensure proper operation of system:

  • Cooling load calculations
  • Technical selection
  • External static pressure calculations for concealed indoor units
  • Noise studies for VIP projects

Heating

We supply and install the following type of air heating systems: Gas Boilers, Oil Boilers, Electric Boilers, Underfloor heating, Radiators, Trench heaters. Prior to installation, we provide you with all the needed engineering studies and calculations to ensure proper operation of system:

  • Heating load calculations
  • Underfloor heating drawing
  • Boilers selection
  • Radiators sizing

Fire fighting

We design, supply and install all components related to fire systems: piping and supports, main fire pumps, jockey pumps, necessary controls and valves as per NFPA. Prior to installation, we provide you with all the needed engineering studies and calculations to ensure proper operation of system:

  • Pipe sizing
  • Hydraulic calculations
  • Pumps selection
  • Fire zoning

Water treatment

We supply, install and test all water treatment systems as follow:

  • Water softeners
  • Reverse Osmosis
  • Dosing pumps and tanks for closed water systems
  • Sand, Carbon and Cartridge filters

Swimming pools

We supply and install all mechanical related components of indoor and outdoor swimming pools such as:

  • Dehumidifiers
  • Chlorination
  • Filtration

Plumbing & Drainage
